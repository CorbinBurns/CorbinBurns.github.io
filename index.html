Minecraft Tips

A Guide To Playing Minecraft

Step 1

What are the controls? How do you load a world?

To start off playing minecraft you have to first load a world. The first step in creating a world is by clicking the single player button on the main menue. Once in singleplayer mode click "Create New World". Then give the world a name and fill out the options. When you first start out you need know that the basic controls. 'w' 'a' 's' and 'd' make you move forward, left, down, and right. Then you can use the mouse to look around. The right mouse button is to place blocks while the left mouse button breaks blocks. 'e' is used to access your inventory where you can craft items and shift is used to crouch.

Step 2

What To Do On Your First Night?

The best thing to do before your first night is get a lot of wood. Once you have around half a stack of wood (32) then you are ready to get tucked away for the night. To find a base for the night you will want to find a cave, in this cave you will be able to mine and do all the boring hard work during the night. You can also find valuable loot in caves if you look hard enough. But be careful even though you're unerground mobs can still spawn.

Step 3

How To Get Gear, And Whats The Best?

To make gear you will first need a crafting table which requires wood, and you will also need iron. Luckily you should have all of this from the night before since you were in the mine all night. The next step is to smelt the iron in a furnace. Then once you have enough iron start crafting the armor. Start off by making an iron breatplate and a sword. Then leggings, boots, and then finally a helmet ifyou have enought iron. Iron is the second best armor and the easiest ore to find. The next level will be diamonds.

Step 4

Making Your Dope House!

The last step in our minecraft adventure is buliding you home, a place of rest and relaxation from killing all the mobs. Your house can be where ever you want it but I would recomend a mountain house or castle. With the mountain house you will need to dig out a type of hole on te side of a mountain and build a kind of deck. You can then do whatever you want with how it looks or how big it is. For the castle you just need a lot of stone and wood. It is best to build the castle on falt land but if you want it to look sick you could put it on top of a mountain. Houses are very easy to make, just make sure they're big and that you like them.
