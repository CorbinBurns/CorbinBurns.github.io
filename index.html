Minecraft Tips

A Guide To Playing Minecraft

Step 1

What are the controls? How do you load a world?

To start off playing minecraft you have to first load a world. The first step in creating a world is by clicking the single player button on the main menue. Once in singleplayer mode click "Create New World". Then give the world a name and fill out the options. When you first start out you need know that the basic controls. 'w' 'a' 's' and 'd' make you move forward, left, down, and right. Then you can use the mouse to look around. The right mouse button is to place blocks while the left mouse button breaks blocks. 'e' is used to access your inventory where you can craft items and shift is used to crouch.

Step 2

What To Do On Your First Night

The best thing to do before your first night is get a lot of wood. Once you have around half a stack of wood (32) then you are ready to get tucked away for the night. To find a base for the night you will want to find a cave, in this cave you will be able to mine and do all the boring hard work during the night. You can also find valuable loot in caves if you look hard enough. But be careful even though you're unerground mobs can still spawn.
